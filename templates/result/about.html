About Us

What you need to know

Founded in 2014, Central University, formerly known as the Canadian University of Modern Technology, proudly embraces educational innovation and excellence. In 2008, Mr. Muckson Sesay established the Ishaak Muckson Innovative Technology Training Center to provide essential hardware and software training for youths in Sierra Leone, fostering a new generation of skilled professionals.

At Central University, we strive towards developing knowledge-based curricula, creating a first-class educational facility with a premier training system. The University has clear and vibrant educational goals.
